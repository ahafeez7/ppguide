MATIN FLavored StackEdit Tutorial

[StackEdit](https://stackedit.io/) is browser embedded Markdown editor with many integrated features that makes life easier for those who do not want to meddle around GitHub too much, or spend time writing in Markdown or HTML. We made life even further easier by creating a specialized instance of StackEdit with the sole purpose of writing posts to Project-Pages. You should still use the original StackEdit for any other application.
